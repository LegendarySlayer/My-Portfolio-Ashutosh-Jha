My Story: From Code to Insights

My journey into technology began with a fascination for building things. I started with web development, learning how to construct robust, user-friendly applications from the ground up. This gave me a strong foundation in software engineering principles and a love for creating clean, scalable code.

However, I soon realized that the most powerful applications were the ones that could think. My curiosity led me to the world of data science and AI, a turning point that was solidified during my international immersion at the National University of Singapore. There, I saw firsthand how raw data could be transformed into predictive models and actionable business intelligence.

Today, I live at the intersection of these two worlds. I don't just build apps; I infuse them with intelligence. I don't just analyze data; I build the platforms to make those insights accessible and interactive. This "Data Weaver" philosophy drives me to create solutions, like the maintenance portal for Tata Steel, that are not only functional but truly smart.
